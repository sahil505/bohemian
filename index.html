Rendezvous is the annual cultural fest of IIT Delhi. It will be held from October 13th till October 16th.

Rendezvous is inimitable product of Delhi’s unique lust for life and BRCA’s proverbial tendency towards excellence. It is a 3 day of extravaganza with equal parts of awesomeness in organization and turnout. We are young, wild, free and exuberant. Come join us to celebrate the joy of life.

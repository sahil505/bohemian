Campaign

The Red War

Thalassemia is a genetic blood disorder characterised by synthesis of abnormal haemoglobin by the body.


Even today, no medicinal cure is present for this dreaded disease and regular blood transfusion is perhaps the most essential requirement.


This campaign is a determined effort to eliminate the widespread ignorance and strive to educate as well as bring about awareness and seriousness to combat Thalassemia.


It is not a battle that claims lives and causes fatalities, it is a war that endeavours to save precious lives that need not be lost. Come join us as we wage this war to save lives and help us make a difference

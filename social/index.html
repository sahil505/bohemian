Cause

This campaign is a determined effort to eliminate the widespread ignorance and strive to educate as well as bring about awareness and seriousness to combat Thalassemia.

Thalassemia is a genetic blood disorder characterised by synthesis of abnormal haemoglobin by the body. Even today, no medicinal cure is present for this dreaded disease and regular blood transfusion is perhaps the most essential requirement

Statistics

Every year approximately 100,000 children with Thalassemia Major are born world over, of which 10,000 are born in India.

100000 thalassemia majors of which 50% will not survive beyond the age of 25.

15000 crore is the amount India spends for thalassemia care every year.

35-40 million carriers across the country , 95% of treatment expenses are borne by the patients.
